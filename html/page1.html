Famous Reaction Examples

Zero-order reaction: Decomposition of N₂O₅

This reaction is an example of a zero-order reaction, where the rate is constant regardless of the concentration of N₂O₅. The reaction proceeds as follows:

N₂O₅ → 2NO + O₂

First-order reaction: Decomposition of NO₂

This reaction is a classic first-order reaction, where the rate depends linearly on the concentration of NO₂. The reaction proceeds as follows:

2NO₂ → 2NO + O₂

Second-order reaction: NO₂ + NO₂ → N₂O₄

This reaction follows second-order kinetics, with the rate depending on the square of the concentration of NO₂. The reaction proceeds as follows:

2NO₂ → N₂O₄
