Chemical Kinetics Visualizer

This interactive tool helps you visualize chemical reactions step-by-step, including kinetics, activation energy, reaction mechanisms, and catalyst effects. It allows users to manipulate variables such as concentration, temperature, and catalysts to observe the effect on reaction rates.
